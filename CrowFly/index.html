Abstract
Poplars and willows are the most widely distributed plants in China. Poplars, in particular, are the most widely distributed and adaptable trees in the world. However, the willow leaf beetle(柳蓝叶甲), a common pest, is quite harmful to both poplars and willows since both the adult and the larva of the willow leaf beetle feed on poplar and willow leaves. What's worse, the leaves may be all eaten up. Luckily, a hero appeared, namely Pseudomonas(假单胞菌), a king of prokaryote(原核生物). Some pseudomonas can kill willow orchid leaf nail. In addition, RNAi, a technology that has won a Nobel Prize, can also be used to resist insects since the last decade. It is also known as the third revolution in pesticides(杀虫剂). However, insect-resistant Pseudomonas and RNAi technologies do not work well when used alone. Our team combined them both, thus a biocontrol agent was obtained which had good control effect on willow orchid leaf nail. This new technique can kill the Plagiodera versicolora and maximize the protection of poplars and willows. This is the synergistic insect-resistant effect of insect-resistant Pseudomonas and RNAi technology. That's the whole research of our team.
